Tech Girls Drive

I volunteered as a speaker for Tech4Dev’s "Tech Girls Drive", a tech-advocacy initiative aimed at enlightening female students on career opportunities in STEM fields, encouraging them to study STEM-related courses, and educating them on the relevance of technology in today’s work culture. During visits to Westside School and Berean Mission International School, I engaged with students, sharing insights and sparking interest in the world of technology. This initiative was instrumental in inspiring young girls to explore and pursue STEM careers.
